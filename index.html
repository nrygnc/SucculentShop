GUIDES TO GREENERY

Hello Succulent lovers!

Echeveria

Echeverias are one of the most popular succulent plants. They come in a range of colors, including pink, red, and blue-gray. They have rosette-shaped leaves and produce tall, colorful flowers on long stems.

Sedum

Sedums are a large group of succulent plants that are known for their thick, fleshy leaves and colorful flowers. They come in a variety of shapes and sizes and can be grown both indoors and outdoors.

String of Pearls

The String of Pearls is a unique succulent plant with long, trailing stems that are covered in small, spherical leaves. It produces small, white flowers on tall stems and is commonly grown as a hanging plant.
